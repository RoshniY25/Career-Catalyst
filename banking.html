🏦 Banking Professional

Banking professionals play a crucial role in managing financial transactions, providing investment guidance, and supporting the economy through various banking services. This career is ideal for commerce students with a knack for finance, business, and customer service.

🎯 Top Roles in Banking

  • Bank Probationary Officer (PO)
  • Bank Clerk / Assistant
  • Investment Banker
  • Credit Analyst
  • Loan Officer
  • Relationship Manager
  • Risk Analyst

📚 Courses & Education Path

  • B.Com (Banking & Finance)
  • BBA in Banking
  • MBA in Finance / Banking
  • Post Graduate Diploma in Banking & Financial Services
  • Certification courses like JAIIB, CAIIB (for professionals)

📝 Major Banking Exams

  • IBPS PO / Clerk: Institute of Banking Personnel Selection for public sector banks
  • SBI PO / Clerk: State Bank of India recruitment exams
  • RBI Grade B: Reserve Bank of India – Officer Level
  • RBI Assistant: Assistant posts in RBI
  • NABARD / SEBI Exams: Development and regulatory sector roles

🌍 Work Opportunities

  • Public Sector Banks (SBI, PNB, BOB, etc.)
  • Private Banks (HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, etc.)
  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
  • Regional Rural Banks
  • International Banks
  • Microfinance and NBFCs

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Strong numerical and analytical ability
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Financial literacy and business knowledge
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ethics and integrity in financial dealings
