Career Details: Commerce Stream

Explore top commerce-based careers in depth

📊 Chartered Accountant (CA)

Description: Handle accounting, auditing, and financial reporting for companies.

Skills: Numbers, attention to detail, finance, tax knowledge

Path: 12th Commerce → CA Foundation → CA Intermediate → CA Final

Average Salary: ₹7–20 LPA

🏢 Business Analyst

Description: Analyze business data to improve processes and profitability.

Skills: Communication, problem-solving, Excel, basic programming

Path: 12th Commerce → BBA/B.Com → MBA/Certifications

Average Salary: ₹6–15 LPA

💼 Investment Banker

Description: Advise companies on investment, mergers, and financial growth.

Skills: Finance, analysis, negotiation, communication

Path: 12th Commerce → B.Com/BBA → MBA (Finance)

Average Salary: ₹10–25 LPA
