📊 Business Analyst

A Business Analyst plays a key role in understanding business needs and helping organizations make data-driven decisions. It is a high-demand profession across industries like finance, IT, marketing, and consulting.

🎯 Role & Responsibilities

  • Analyze data to improve business processes
  • Communicate with stakeholders and gather requirements
  • Create reports, dashboards, and visualizations
  • Recommend solutions using data trends and forecasting
  • Work with IT teams to implement business solutions

📚 Courses to Become a Business Analyst

  • BBA / BMS (Bachelor of Business Administration/Studies)
  • B.Com with specialization in Business Analytics
  • Bachelor in Economics or Statistics
  • Professional Certifications (IIBA, PMI-PBA, ECBA)
  • Postgraduate Options: MBA (Business Analytics), M.Sc. in Data Analytics

📝 Entrance Exams

  • CUET UG: For BBA/BMS/B.Com programs in central universities
  • NPAT: NMIMS Programs
  • IPMAT: IIM Indore & IIM Rohtak (5-Year Integrated MBA)
  • DUET: For select B.Com/BBA programs at Delhi University
  • CAT / XAT / CMAT: For MBA after graduation

🌍 Top Colleges for Business Analytics / BBA

  • IIM Indore, IIM Rohtak (IPM Program)
  • NMIMS Mumbai
  • Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (DU)
  • Christ University, Bangalore
  • Symbiosis Centre for Management Studies

💼 Skills Required

  • Analytical thinking
  • Excel and data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau)
  • Communication & business acumen
  • Basic knowledge of SQL and statistics
  • Problem-solving & decision-making
