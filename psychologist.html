🧠 Career in Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of mind and behavior. As a psychologist, you help people understand and cope with mental health challenges, behavior issues, or emotional well-being.

🎓 Courses After 12th

  • B.A. / B.Sc. in Psychology
  • B.A. in Applied Psychology
  • B.A. + M.A. Integrated Psychology Programs
  • Diploma in Counseling / Clinical Psychology
  • Certification in Child Psychology, Behavioral Science

📝 Entrance Exams

  • CUET-UG – Central Universities for Psychology programs
  • DUET (Now via CUET) – Delhi University entrance
  • IPU CET – Guru Gobind Singh IP University
  • JMI Entrance Test – Jamia Millia Islamia
  • Private University Exams – Amity, Christ, Ashoka, etc.

🏫 Top Psychology Institutes in India

  • University of Delhi (LSR, Jesus & Mary, Daulat Ram)
  • Christ University, Bangalore
  • Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS), Mumbai
  • Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi
  • Amity University
  • Fergusson College, Pune

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Empathy and compassion
  • Active listening and patience
  • Strong observational and analytical skills
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Communication and interpersonal abilities
