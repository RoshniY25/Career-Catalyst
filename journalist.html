📰 Career in Journalism & Mass Communication

Journalism is a dynamic career for those who are passionate about storytelling, truth-seeking, and influencing society through various media platforms like print, TV, and digital.

🎓 Courses After 12th

  • Bachelor of Journalism (BJ)
  • Bachelor of Mass Communication (BMC)
  • B.A. in Journalism & Mass Media
  • Diploma in Journalism or Digital Media
  • Integrated B.A. + M.A. in Mass Communication

📝 Entrance Exams

  • IIMC Entrance Exam – Indian Institute of Mass Communication
  • CUET-UG – For central universities offering journalism courses
  • JMI Entrance Test – Jamia Millia Islamia's Mass Comm programs
  • SET – For Symbiosis Centre for Media & Communication
  • XIC OET – Xavier Institute of Communications Online Test

🏫 Top Journalism Institutes in India

  • IIMC (Delhi & Regional Centres)
  • AJK Mass Communication Research Centre (JMI)
  • Symbiosis Institute of Media & Communication (Pune)
  • Asian College of Journalism (Chennai)
  • Delhi University – IP College for Women, LSR
  • Xavier Institute of Communications (Mumbai)

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Excellent verbal and written communication
  • Strong research and investigative skills
  • Ethics and integrity
  • Critical thinking and objectivity
  • Basic knowledge of multimedia tools
