🎨 Arts & Humanities Entrance Exams

Here's a list of major entrance exams for students pursuing Arts, Humanities, and Social Sciences after 10+2.

📚 General Humanities & Social Sciences

  • CUET (UG): Entrance exam for B.A., B.A. (Hons), Political Science, History, Psychology, etc.
  • IP College Entrance: For Journalism and B.A. (Hons) programs in Delhi University.
  • TISS BAT: TISS Bachelor’s Admission Test for Social Work and Liberal Arts.

📝 Journalism & Mass Communication

  • IIMC Entrance Exam: For B.A. in Journalism & Mass Communication.
  • CUET (UG): For B.A. Journalism/Mass Media courses in central universities.
  • IPU CET: Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Journalism entrance test.

⚖️ Law (5-year integrated)

  • CLAT: Common Law Admission Test for top National Law Universities.
  • LSAT-India: Law School Admission Test for private law colleges.
  • SLAT: Symbiosis Law Admission Test.

🎭 Fine Arts, Design & Performing Arts

  • NID DAT: Design Aptitude Test for B.Des at NID.
  • UCEED: Undergraduate Common Entrance Exam for Design (IITs).
  • FTII & SRFTI Entrance: For Film, Acting & Performing Arts courses.

🗺️ Language & Literature

  • CUET (UG): For English, Hindi, Sanskrit, and other language courses.
  • JNU Entrance (now CUET): For foreign language programs like French, German, Japanese, etc.
