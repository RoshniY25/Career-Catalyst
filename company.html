📋 Company Secretary (CS)

A Company Secretary ensures legal compliance and corporate governance in organizations. CS professionals work with company boards, manage regulatory filings, and handle legal aspects related to business operations. It is one of the most respected careers in the commerce field.

🎯 Key Responsibilities

  • Ensure compliance with corporate laws and regulations
  • Handle company board meetings and records
  • File legal documents with regulatory authorities
  • Advise management on corporate governance matters
  • Coordinate between stakeholders, board, and government bodies

📚 CS Course Levels

  • CSEET: Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test
  • CS Executive: After passing CSEET (Graduates may be exempt)
  • CS Professional: After completing Executive level

📌 Eligibility & Duration

  • 10+2 passed students can enroll in CSEET
  • Graduates (in any stream except Fine Arts) can directly join Executive level
  • CS course duration is approx. 3–4 years (including training)

🌍 Career Opportunities

  • Company Secretary in MNCs and listed companies
  • Compliance Officer / Corporate Legal Advisor
  • Practicing CS (self-employed professional)
  • Work with SEBI, Stock Exchanges, Law Firms
  • Board-level corporate consultant

🛠️ Skills Required

  • In-depth knowledge of laws and governance
  • Communication and drafting skills
  • Organizational and leadership qualities
  • Integrity and professional ethics
