🔬 Career in Scientific Research

Research careers are ideal for curious minds passionate about discovery, innovation, and solving real-world problems. Whether in labs, universities, or tech companies — research drives the future.

🧠 Fields of Research

  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Biological & Life Sciences (Biotech, Microbiology, Genetics)
  • Environmental & Climate Science
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • Medical & Clinical Research
  • Agricultural & Food Science

📚 Courses After 12th

  • B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
  • B.Sc. (Hons) + M.Sc. in Research streams
  • Integrated M.Sc. (IISER, NISER, CUET)
  • B.Stat / B.Math (ISI, IITs)
  • Dual Degree Programs (BS-MS)

📝 Entrance Exams

  • IISER Aptitude Test (IAT)
  • NEST – National Entrance Screening Test
  • CUET-UG – Central Universities Entrance Test
  • KVPY (Discontinued but replaced by IAT/NEST)
  • ISI Admission Test – For B.Stat/B.Math

🏫 Top Research Institutes in India

  • IISc Bangalore
  • IISERs (Pune, Mohali, Bhopal, Kolkata, Thiruvananthapuram)
  • NISER Bhubaneswar
  • TIFR – Tata Institute of Fundamental Research
  • ISI Kolkata – Indian Statistical Institute

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Analytical and logical thinking
  • Curiosity and critical observation
  • Mathematical and scientific aptitude
  • Attention to detail
  • Good writing & documentation skills
