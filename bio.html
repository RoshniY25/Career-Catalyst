🧬 Biology (PCB) Stream

The Biology stream is ideal for students interested in life sciences, medicine, and healthcare. It opens up career paths in medical, research, pharma, biotechnology, and many more.

🎯 Top Career Options

  • Doctor (MBBS, BDS, Ayurveda, Homeopathy)
  • Veterinarian (B.VSc)
  • Pharmacist (B.Pharm)
  • Microbiologist, Biotechnologist
  • Forensic Scientist
  • Botanist / Zoologist
  • Psychologist / Clinical Researcher
  • Healthcare Management / Nursing

📚 Popular Courses After 12th (PCB)

  • MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery
  • BDS - Dental Surgery
  • BAMS - Ayurvedic Medicine
  • BHMS - Homeopathic Medicine
  • BPT - Physiotherapy
  • B.Sc in Biology, Microbiology, Genetics, Biotechnology
  • BNYS - Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences
  • B.VSc - Veterinary Science
  • Nursing / Paramedical Courses

📝 Major Entrance Exams

  • NEET-UG: Mandatory for MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, Veterinary, Nursing, and Allied Health Sciences.
  • AIIMS & JIPMER: Merged into NEET-UG (for top institutes).
  • CUET (for B.Sc programs): Central University Entrance Test.
  • ICAR AIEEA: For B.Sc Agriculture and Allied Sciences.
  • IPU CET / CUCET / Other University Exams: For UG Biology-related programs.

🌍 State-wise Medical Entrance Exams (via NEET)

Almost all states now use NEET for medical admissions, but counseling is state-managed:

🔸 Maharashtra

Admissions through NEET-UG via MHT-CET Medical Counseling.

🔸 Tamil Nadu

Medical seats are filled through NEET, managed by TN Health Department.

🔸 Kerala

Admission via NEET and KEAM counseling (for allied courses).

🔸 Uttar Pradesh

Medical counseling conducted by UPDGME after NEET.

🔸 Karnataka

State-level counseling handled by KEA after NEET results.

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Analytical skills
