🏰 Career in Law

Law is an excellent field for individuals passionate about justice, legal rights, and public service. A career in law offers diverse opportunities such as practicing law, corporate legal advising, judicial services, and civil services.

📖 Courses After 12th

  • 5-Year Integrated Law Programs (B.A. LL.B, B.Com LL.B, BBA LL.B)
  • 3-Year LL.B (after graduation)
  • Diploma in Cyber Law, Labor Law, Intellectual Property Law

🗒️ Entrance Exams

  • CLAT – Common Law Admission Test (National Law Universities)
  • LSAT India – Law School Admission Test for private universities
  • SLAT – Symbiosis Law Admission Test
  • AILET – All India Law Entrance Test (NLU Delhi)
  • MH CET Law – Maharashtra State Law Entrance

🏨 Career Options in Law

  • Litigation Lawyer
  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Judge (through Judicial Services Exam)
  • Legal Advisor (Private/NGO/Government)
  • Public Prosecutor
  • Civil Services (with legal background)

🏫 Top Law Colleges in India

  • NLSIU Bangalore
  • NLU Delhi
  • NALSAR Hyderabad
  • WBNUJS Kolkata
  • Symbiosis Law School (Pune)
  • Faculty of Law, DU

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Strong communication & reasoning skills
  • Analytical thinking & critical interpretation
  • Good knowledge of current affairs
  • Ability to argue logically
  • Confidence and ethics
