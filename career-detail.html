📘 Entrance Exams for Science Stream Careers

Here is a list of important entrance exams after 12th science to pursue careers in Engineering, Medical, Pharmacy, and Research.

⚙️ Engineering Entrance Exams

  • JEE Main & JEE Advanced – For admission to NITs, IIITs, and IITs.
  • BITSAT – BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses.
  • VITEEE – VIT Vellore and other campuses.
  • SRMJEEE – SRM University campuses.
  • State-level CETs – MHT CET (MH), KCET (KA), AP EAMCET, etc.

🧬 Medical Entrance Exams

  • NEET-UG – For MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, BPT, Nursing, etc.
  • AIIMS Delhi (via NEET) – Prestigious institute for medical education.
  • JIPMER Puducherry (via NEET) – Another top medical institution.
  • State Counseling Exams – Through NEET score for state quota seats.

💊 Pharmacy Entrance Exams

  • GPAT (Postgraduate) – For M.Pharm admissions.
  • State CET Exams – MHT CET, WBJEE, TS EAMCET, etc. for B.Pharm.
  • BITSAT / VITEEE / SRMJEEE – Offer B.Pharm programs.

🔬 Research & Pure Science Entrance Exams

  • IISER Aptitude Test (IAT) – For admission to 5-year BS-MS programs.
  • NEST – For NISER and CEBS Mumbai.
  • CUET-UG – For central universities like DU, BHU, etc.
  • ISI Admission Test – For B.Stat and B.Math programs.
  • KVPY (Discontinued) – Replaced by IAT/NEST.

📍 State-Wise Common Entrance Tests (CETs)

  • MHT CET – Maharashtra (Engg, Pharmacy, Agriculture)
  • KCET – Karnataka (Engg, Pharma, Vet Science)
  • TS EAMCET / AP EAMCET – Telangana / Andhra Pradesh
  • WBJEE – West Bengal Joint Entrance Exam
  • GUJCET – Gujarat CET for Engg and Pharmacy
