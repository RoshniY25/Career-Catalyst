🎨 Career in Graphic Designing

Graphic Designing blends creativity, communication, and technology to convey ideas through visual elements. From branding to digital content, it's a key part of media, marketing, and entertainment industries.

🎓 Courses After 12th

  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des) – Communication / Graphic Design
  • B.Sc in Multimedia / Animation / Visual Communication
  • Diploma in Graphic Design / Animation / Web Design
  • Certificate courses in Adobe Creative Suite, UI/UX
  • Advanced Diploma in 2D/3D Animation and Motion Graphics

📝 Entrance Exams

  • NID DAT – National Institute of Design Admission Test
  • UCEED – Undergraduate Common Entrance Exam for Design (IITs)
  • PEARL / SID / MIT DAT – Private university design entrance tests
  • CUET-UG – For some B.Voc and B.A. Design programs

🏫 Top Institutes for Graphic Design in India

  • National Institute of Design (NID)
  • MIT Institute of Design, Pune
  • Symbiosis Institute of Design
  • National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)
  • Pearl Academy
  • Srishti Institute of Art, Design & Technology

🛠️ Skills Required

  • Creative thinking and originality
  • Understanding of color theory, typography, and layout
  • Proficiency in tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma
  • Visual storytelling and branding
  • UI/UX and digital interface design basics
